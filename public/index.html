About Us

MR. COD Has been a team since 2014, we originally started out as an FLL team and in 2019 we decided to make the change to become an FTC team. We are situated in Middleton, Wisconsin and work closely with a non-profit known as Badger Bots which supplies a majority of our equipment and serves as a meeting space. Here at MR. COD, we try to mix fun, learning, and competition. We currently have 8 members in this 2022-2023 season. Our mission has been to spread STEM throughout communities with high minority rates to inspire disadvantaged youth to involve themselves in STEM. We take pride in knowing that we are advocators of both STEM and FIRST within our community.

To us, gracious professionalism is a way of showing respect to all those around you and understanding the value that each individual has. In more of a FTC related sense, gracious professionalism is all about treating everyone kindly, whether they are an alliance member or a team we go up against. We think that gracious professionalism also promotes the idea that things can remain competitive while also being friendly, which can really help develop the community.
